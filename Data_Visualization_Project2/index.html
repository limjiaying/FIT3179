World Population

The world population currently stands at 7.98 billion , and is estimated to hit 8 billion by end of the year 2022. The world's population continues to increase by roughly 140 people per minute, with births outweighing deaths in most countries.

The population density of a country is the number of population per unit of total land area of the country. It can affect a country in different ways such as economic growth qnd environmental impact.

The population of Asia has took up more than 50% of the world's population percentage. It is clear that India and China are the countries that contributes the most to this.
